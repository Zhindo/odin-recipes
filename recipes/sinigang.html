Pork Sinigang



Hot sour broth filled with pork, taro, lady fingers, tomato, water spinach, and long green peppers

Pork Sinigang is the one of the foods that are very liked by the Filipinos with its many varieties. And in this recipe, we will showcase the variety of sinigang which has pork in it..


    Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. pork belly sliced into cubes
  • 2 to 3 cups water spinach leaves
  • 3 pieces long green chili pepper
  • 2 pieces tomato sliced into 4 pieces
  • 1 cup sliced daikon radish
  • 10 to 12 pieces of snake beans cut in 2 inch lengths
  • 1 piece onion sliced thinly
  • 15 pieces okra
  • 4 pieces taro peeled and cut in half
  • 8 cups water
  • 2 packs Knorr Sinigang sa Sampaloc Mix Original 20 gram pack
  • 3 tbsp. fish sauce
  • 1/4 tsp. ground black pepper

    Steps

  1. Pour water into a cooking pot. Let boil
  2. Add onion and tomato. Boil for 5 to 8 minutes
  3. Add the taro. Cover and continue to boil for 30 minutes.
  4. Add Knorr Sinigang sa Sampaloc mix, fish sauce, and ground black pepper. Stir
  5. Put the daikon radish in. Cook for 3 to 5 minutes.
  6. Add the chili peppers, lady fingers, and snake beans. Cook for 7 minutes.
  7. Add the kangkong. Cover the cooking pot and turn off the heat. Let it stay covered for 5 minutes.
  8. Transfer to as serving bowl. Serve with a small bowl with fish sauce and spicy chili. Share and enjoy!
